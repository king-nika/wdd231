Personal Recipe Collection: Site plan

Site Name

DelishBook - This name was chosen because it combines "delicious" and "book," representing a curated collection of tasty recipes, much like a recipe notebook or digital cookbook.

Optional domain: delishbook.net

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide users with a dynamic, interactive, and accessible personal recipe collection. It enables browsing curated recipes, viewing detailed information like ingredients and preparation steps, and submitting new suggestionsâ€”all within a sleek, responsive interface.

Scenarios

  • How can I quickly find a vegetarian or easy-to-make recipe from my personal collection?
  • Can I add a new recipe or mark one as a favorite so I can return to it later?

Color Schema

  • Dark Cerulean (#205375): Used for headings, buttons, and emphasis.
  • Deep Purple (#6a1b9a): Used for highlights, accents, and section labels.

Typography

  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for headings to give a classy, elegant touch appropriate for food and recipe content .
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for body text to ensure clean, easy-to-read paragraphs and descriptions.
